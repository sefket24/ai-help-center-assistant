# Account Management

## Changing Your Email Address

To change your email, go to **Settings > Profile > Contact Information** and click **Edit** next to your email. Enter the new address and confirm it. A verification link will be sent to the new email — you must click it before the change takes effect.

## Changing Your Password

Go to **Settings > Security > Change Password**. Enter your current password, then your new password twice. Passwords must be at least 8 characters and include at least one number and one special character.

## Managing Team Members

Owners and Admins can invite team members under **Settings > Team > Members**. Click **Invite Member**, enter their email, and select a role (Admin, Editor, or Viewer). Invitees receive an email with a link to join. Pending invitations can be cancelled from the same page.

## Roles and Permissions

- **Owner**: Full access, including billing and account deletion. Only one owner per account.
- **Admin**: Can manage members, settings, and content, but cannot access billing or delete the account.
- **Editor**: Can create and edit content but cannot manage members or settings.
- **Viewer**: Read-only access to content.

## Transferring Account Ownership

Go to **Settings > Team > Members**, click the three-dot menu next to the member you want to make Owner, and select **Transfer Ownership**. You will be downgraded to Admin after the transfer.

## Deactivating Your Account

To temporarily deactivate your account, go to **Settings > Account > Deactivate**. Your data is preserved and you can reactivate by logging back in within 90 days. After 90 days, deactivated accounts are permanently deleted.

## Deleting Your Account

Account deletion is permanent. Go to **Settings > Account > Delete Account**, confirm by entering your password, and click **Delete**. All data will be removed within 30 days. This action cannot be undone.

## Single Sign-On (SSO)

Enterprise plans support SSO via SAML 2.0. Contact your account manager or support to begin the SSO setup process. Once configured, team members can log in using your company's identity provider.

## Audit Log

Admins and Owners can view an audit log of all account activity under **Settings > Security > Audit Log**. The log retains 90 days of history and can be exported as a CSV.
