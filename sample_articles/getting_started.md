# Getting Started

## Creating Your Account

To create an account, visit our website and click **Sign Up**. Enter your name, email address, and a secure password. You'll receive a confirmation email — click the link inside to verify your address and activate your account.

## Logging In

Go to the login page and enter your email and password. If you've forgotten your password, click **Forgot Password** and we'll send a reset link to your email address within a few minutes.

## Setting Up Your Profile

After logging in, navigate to **Settings > Profile** to add your name, profile picture, and time zone. Keeping your time zone accurate ensures notifications and scheduled items appear at the correct local time.

## Installing the Desktop App

Download the desktop app from our website under **Downloads**. The app is available for Windows 10+ and macOS 12+. After installing, sign in with your existing account credentials — no new account needed.

## Mobile App

Our mobile app is available on iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play). Search for our product name and install the official app. Sign in with the same email and password you use on the web.

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

We strongly recommend enabling 2FA for added security. Go to **Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication** and follow the steps to connect an authenticator app (such as Google Authenticator or Authy).

## Getting Help

If you run into issues during setup, check the troubleshooting section below or contact our support team via the in-app chat or by emailing support@example.com.
