# Troubleshooting

## App Won't Load

1. Clear your browser cache and cookies, then reload the page.
2. Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Edge are recommended).
3. Disable browser extensions, especially ad blockers, as they can interfere with the app.
4. Check our status page at status.example.com for any active incidents.

## Login Issues

**Incorrect password**: Use the **Forgot Password** link on the login page to reset it.

**Account locked**: After 10 failed login attempts, accounts are locked for 30 minutes. Wait and try again, or reset your password.

**No confirmation email**: Check your spam folder. If it's not there, go to the login page, enter your email, and request a new confirmation email.

## Slow Performance

- Make sure you're using a supported browser (Chrome 100+, Firefox 100+, Safari 15+, Edge 100+).
- Check your internet connection — we recommend at least 5 Mbps for a smooth experience.
- Close unused tabs and applications to free up memory.
- If performance issues persist, try clearing your browser cache.

## File Upload Errors

- Maximum file size is 100 MB per file.
- Supported formats: PDF, DOCX, XLSX, PNG, JPG, GIF, MP4, and CSV.
- Make sure the file is not open in another application before uploading.
- If you see "Upload failed", check your internet connection and try again.

## Notifications Not Arriving

- Check that notifications are enabled under **Settings > Notifications**.
- For email notifications, check your spam folder and add notifications@example.com to your contacts.
- For browser notifications, make sure you've granted permission in your browser settings.
- For mobile push notifications, check that the app has notification permission in your device settings.

## Data Not Syncing

If changes you make on one device don't appear on another:
1. Pull down to refresh on mobile, or press Ctrl+Shift+R (Cmd+Shift+R on Mac) on the web.
2. Log out and back in on the affected device.
3. Check that both devices are connected to the internet.

## Contacting Support

If the steps above don't resolve your issue, please contact our support team:
- **In-app chat**: Click the chat icon in the bottom-right corner.
- **Email**: support@example.com
- **Response time**: Within 4 hours on business days (Mon–Fri, 9 AM–6 PM ET).
