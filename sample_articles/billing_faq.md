# Billing FAQ

## What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), as well as PayPal. For annual plans, we also accept bank transfers and purchase orders for qualifying business accounts.

## When will I be charged?

Your billing cycle starts on the day you subscribe. For monthly plans, you are charged on the same date each month. For annual plans, you are charged once per year on your subscription anniversary date.

## How do I update my payment method?

Go to **Settings > Billing > Payment Methods** and click **Add Payment Method**. Once the new card is added, set it as the default. You can then remove the old card if desired.

## Can I get a refund?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions. If you cancel within 14 days of your first charge and have not exceeded plan limits, contact support and we'll issue a full refund. After 14 days, refunds are not available for partial billing periods.

## What happens if my payment fails?

If a payment fails, we'll notify you by email and retry the charge over the next 7 days. During this grace period your account remains active. If the payment continues to fail, your account will be downgraded to the free tier until billing is resolved.

## How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to **Settings > Billing > Subscription** and click **Cancel Subscription**. Your plan will remain active until the end of the current billing period, after which your account moves to the free tier. Data is retained for 30 days after cancellation.

## Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or students?

Yes. Nonprofits with valid 501(c)(3) status receive a 50% discount. Students with a valid .edu email address receive 30% off. Apply through **Settings > Billing > Discounts** or contact support with your documentation.

## Where can I find my invoices?

All invoices are available under **Settings > Billing > Invoice History**. You can download PDF copies for your records or for expense reimbursement.
